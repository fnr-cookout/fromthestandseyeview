From The Stand's Eyeview

Inspiration is ubiquitous, at least it's how i see.

A shade of green. The weight of someone's silence. The way ambition and tenderness can live in the same chest without killing each other. I collect these things involuntarily, endlessly, with no real plan for what to do with all of it.

Then I write.

Not because the world is dark and needs exposing. But because it's extraordinary and keeps getting mistaken for ordinary. There is love buried in places people have given up digging. There is beauty in things that haven't been named yet.

I believe that.

I have to.

— your friendly neighborhood rapper —
